A pocket of Asian culture with a celebrity following, Taste of Asia in Jesmond is all set to launch the Taste of Asia @Home Experience, delivering food to various areas in and around Newcastle. Taste of Asia brings to the table food packed with traditional flavour, a vibrant and inventive cocktail menu adds a modern twist, and a flamboyant dining room festooned with Asian lanterns. The Taste of Asia an Asian twist on a fast-food classic comes highly recommended. Watch this space for the launch of the home experience.
